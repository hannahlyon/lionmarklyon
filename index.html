Lion Mark Lyon

International Director 2018-2020

About Mark

Lion Mark views the opportunity to serve our Association as an International Director with the same determination and passion that he has in serving his local community. He is qualified to serve in this position, and is wholly committed to leading by example and to representing all Lions in accordance with our International Purpose and Code of Ethics. As International Director for the 2018 – 2020 term, Lion Mark is confident in his abilities to represent our Association, and pledges to do everything in his power to excel at each of the leadership principles expected to be demonstrated by an International Director.
